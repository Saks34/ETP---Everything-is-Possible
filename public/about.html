About Me

Saksham Satnalika
Location: West Bengal
Education: 2nd Year B.TECH, Lovely Professional University, Punjab
Minor: Full Stack Development

About Me:
Greetings! I am Saksham Satnalika, a tech enthusiast and educator from the culturally rich state of West Bengal. Currently in my second year pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Technology at Lovely Professional University, Punjab, I am deeply passionate about leveraging technology to empower and educate others.

YouTube Journey:
My journey on YouTube commenced in 2019, when I embarked on a mission to share my knowledge and passion for history with fellow classmates. Catering to the needs of class 9 students, I delved into the intricacies of historical events, aiming to make learning both engaging and accessible. Building upon this foundation, in 2020, I expanded my scope to include physics and chemistry for classes 9 and 10, further enriching the educational landscape of my channel. However, in pursuit of academic excellence, I temporarily suspended my YouTube endeavors to focus on my studies.

Academic Achievements:
Driven by a quest for knowledge and academic excellence, I dedicated myself to preparing for the prestigious JEE Mains exam. Through diligence and determination, I successfully cleared the examination, earning the opportunity to sit for the esteemed JEE Advanced exam. With a commendable performance, I secured a remarkable rank of 12589, a testament to my academic prowess and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Skills:
With a robust foundation in various programming languages and technologies, coupled with a flair for effective communication and teaching, I possess the versatility and expertise to navigate the dynamic landscapes of both academia and technology.

Objective:
Armed with a passion for learning and a drive to make a positive impact, I am committed to leveraging my skills and knowledge to contribute meaningfully to the realms of education and technology. With a steadfast focus on personal and professional growth, I aspire to continue innovating, inspiring, and empowering others on my journey towards excellence.
